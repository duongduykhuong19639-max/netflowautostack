# Netflowautostack.com Website Specification

## Project Overview
Professional team website for an app development company targeting global markets (Europe & America)

## Team Information
- **Team Name:** netflowautostack.com
- **Business Support:** support@netflowautostack.com
- **Contact:** contact@netflowautostack.com
- **Address:** Hoa Lac High-Tech Park, Hanoi, Vietnam

## Design Style
- Tesla-inspired modern, clean design
- Light color scheme
- Rich animations and transitions
- Professional corporate aesthetic

## Color Palette
- Primary Background: #FFFFFF
- Secondary Background: #F5F7FA
- Primary Text: #1A1A2E
- Secondary Text: #666666
- Accent Blue: #0066CC
- Accent Gradient: linear-gradient(135deg, #667eea 0%, #764ba2 100%)
- Border Color: #E5E7EB
- Hover Effects: rgba(0, 102, 204, 0.1)

## Typography
- Primary Font: 'Inter', -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', Roboto, sans-serif
- Headings: Bold, tracking-tight
- Body: Regular, 16px base, 1.6 line-height

## Pages Structure
1. **index.html** - Homepage
2. **services.html** - Business Services
3. **culture.html** - Team Culture
4. **news.html** - Team Updates
5. **contact.html** - Contact Us
6. **privacy.html** - Privacy Policy (Global Compliance)
7. **terms.html** - Terms of Service (Global Compliance)

## Technical Stack
- HTML5 (Semantic markup)
- CSS3 (Modern features, Flexbox, Grid, Animations)
- Vanilla JavaScript (ES6+)
- SVG (All icons and graphics)
- PNG (Hero images where needed)
